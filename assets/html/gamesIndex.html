Formats

Draft

At the beginning of a booster draft, each player opens a booster pack and chooses one card. Then everyone passes the remaining cards in the pack to the player to their left. Each player then picks another card from the pack before passing it on again. You keep doing this until all the cards from the booster packs have found an owner. Next, each player opens their second booster pack, but this time the remaining cards are passed to the right. After all the cards have been dealt, the third booster pack is opened and this time passed to the left again. (The illustration above, shows how the packs are passed). After all three packs have been drafted, each player builds a deck from the cards they have chosen. In doing so, each player may add as many standard lands (plains, islands, swamps, mountains, and forests) as they wish, but each deck must have at least 40 cards.

Standard

Standard is a rotating format where you build decks with cards from the current Magic sets. Evolving gameplay and fresh strategies make this format one of the most popular ways to at home and at events like Friday Night Magic.

HOW DO I BUILD A STANDARD DECK?

  • Your deck must contain at least 60 cards.
  • If you use a sideboard, it may contain up to 15 cards.
  • A card may not be present more than 4 times in the main deck and sideboard combined (standard lands excluded).
  • There is no maximum deck size as long as you can still mix the deck yourself with your hands.

The Game:

  • PvP
  • 2-8 players
  • Players start the game with 20 life points
  • The turn order is clockwise
  • The last remaining player wins

Commander

The Commander format is all about choosing a hero and building a deck around him. In this casual multiplayer format, you choose a legendary creature as your commander and build the rest of your deck according to its color identity.

Your deck:

  • 1 commander card (legendary creature)
  • 99 other cards
  • Only one copy of each card, except for standard lands
  • All cards must match the color identity of the commander

The Game:

  • 2-6 players
  • Multiplayer in the format "everyone-against-each".
  • Players start the game with 40 life points
  • All commanders start in their command zone
  • The turn order is clockwise
  • The last remaining player wins
